Puck firmware installer

Connect your puck via usb cable and click connect

Instructions

  • Disconnect all the plugged in devices, only plug in the puck
  • Click connect, a dialogue box will appear, select entry resembling "com6"...
  • Once device is connected, a pop up will ask you "install firmware" click on that, and wait for it to finish
  • Do not unplug device while it is updating.
